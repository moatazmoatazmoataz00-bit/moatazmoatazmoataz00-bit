What is Trivia Party?

Trivia Party is a free, real-time multiplayer trivia game designed to bring people together through the fun and excitement of knowledge-based competition. Whether you're hosting a virtual game night with friends, organizing team-building activities for your workplace, or enhancing learning in a classroom environment, Trivia Party provides an engaging and interactive experience that's both entertaining and educational.

How Does Trivia Party Work?

The game is simple yet thrilling. One player creates a game room and shares the unique room code with other participants. Players join the room by entering the code, and the host configures the game settings‚Äîchoosing the number of rounds (3 to 25) and selecting quiz categories or mixing them all up. Once the host starts the game, all players face the same multiple-choice trivia questions simultaneously. Your score depends on two factors: answering correctly and answering quickly. Speed matters‚Äîthe faster you answer, the more bonus points you earn! After each question, the leaderboard updates in real-time, showing who's winning. Once all rounds are complete, final rankings and scores are displayed, giving everyone a clear view of who triumphed.
