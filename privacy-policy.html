Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Trivia Party ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Trivia Party website and gaming platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy while maintaining an excellent gaming experience.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Username: When you create a room or join a game, you provide a username for identification purposes in multiplayer sessions
  • Game Data: Your answers, scores, performance metrics, and completion times during gameplay
  • Contact Information: If you contact us, we may collect your email address and message content

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, game rooms accessed, and interaction patterns
  • IP Address: Your IP address for server communication and technical troubleshooting
  • Cookies & Local Storage: We use cookies and browser storage to remember preferences and maintain sessions
  • Analytics: Third-party analytics services track aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve our service
  • Referrer Information: How you accessed our site

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the Trivia Party service and gaming experience
  • Enabling real-time multiplayer gameplay, room functionality, and leaderboard features
  • Personalizing your gaming experience based on your preferences
  • Providing customer support and responding to your inquiries
  • Analyzing usage patterns and player behavior to enhance game features
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues, fraud, and abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms of service
  • Sending administrative, security, and service-related notifications
  • Displaying appropriate advertisements through ad networks

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

Trivia Party displays online advertisements provided by Google AdSense and other ad networks. These ad providers and their data practices work as follows:

  • Google AdSense: Uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads and measure advertising effectiveness
  • DoubleClick Cookies: Google uses DoubleClick cookies to track advertising-related activities and prevent ad fraud
  • Personalized Ads: Google may use your browsing history to show you personalized advertisements, subject to your privacy settings
  • Third-Party Ad Networks: Other ad partners may display ads on our site and use cookies for targeting and analytics
  • Ad Targeting: Google and our ad partners do not use sensitive information (health, religion, politics, etc.) for targeted advertising compliance with policies

Ad networks operate under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Cookies.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who assist with hosting, analytics, customer support, and technical infrastructure
  • Ad Networks: Limited data sharing with advertising partners like Google for ad serving and analytics
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or valid legal process
  • Safety & Security: To protect against fraud, security threats, or physical harm to users
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or asset sale of our company
  • Public Display: Usernames and scores may appear on in-game leaderboards that are visible to other players

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law:

  • Session data is typically deleted upon game completion or logout
  • Usernames and scores may be retained for leaderboard purposes for a limited time
  • Usage analytics are aggregated and anonymized after collection
  • Cookies expire based on their intended purpose (session, preference, or analytics)
  • You can request deletion of personal data at any time by contacting us
  • Backup data may be retained for up to 90 days for system recovery purposes

7. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission between your browser and our servers
  • Secure server infrastructure with industry-standard protections
  • Limited employee and contractor access to personal data, restricted to business needs
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Compliance with web security best practices

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use our service at your own risk.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request updates or corrections to inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request erasure of personal data (with limited exceptions)
  • Opt-Out: Disable cookies or opt out of analytics tracking through your browser settings
  • Data Portability: Request personal data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us here or email privacy@triviagame.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can control ad personalization and opt out of tracking:

  • Google Ad Settings: Visit Google Ad Settings to manage personalized ads from Google
  • Network Advertising Initiative: Visit NAI Opt-Out to opt out of participating ad networks
  • Browser Controls: Most browsers allow you to disable cookies or manage cookie permissions
  • Global Privacy Control: If your browser supports GPC (Global Privacy Control), we honor these signals

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

10. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Trivia Party is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we've collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. Parents who believe their child's information has been collected can contact us immediately at privacy@triviagame.com.

11. International Users

If you are located outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in the US or other countries where our servers are located. By using Trivia Party, you consent to this transfer. Users in the European Union and other jurisdictions may have additional data protection rights.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Union Users)

For users in the European Union and EEA countries:

  • We process personal data on the lawful basis of your consent and legitimate interests
  • You have the right to withdraw consent at any time
  • You have the right to file complaints with your local data protection authority
  • We have appointed a Data Protection Officer for GDPR matters and inquiries
  • We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including Articles 15-22
  • Data transfers to the US are based on appropriate safeguards

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have specific privacy rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to know what personal information is being collected and used
  • Right to delete personal information (with certain exceptions)
  • Right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • Right to limit use and disclosure of sensitive personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

We do not sell personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us.

14. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal information or continuing to their sites.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, when required, by posting a prominent notice on our website or sending you an email. Your continued use of Trivia Party after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

16. No Real Money Transactions

Important: Trivia Party is completely free to play and contains absolutely no real-money transactions, gambling, or financial exchanges of any kind. Your gameplay and leaderboard standings are for entertainment purposes only.

17. Contact Us for Privacy Inquiries

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, your personal data, or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@triviagame.com
  • Contact Form: Use our contact form here
  • Response Time: We typically respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days

This Privacy Policy is effective as of February 8, 2026. By using Trivia Party, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.
