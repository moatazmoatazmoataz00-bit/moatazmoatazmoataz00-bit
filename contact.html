Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trivia Party free to play?

Yes! Trivia Party is completely free to play. There are no subscription fees, in-app purchases, or real-money transactions. No hidden charges.

Can I play offline?

Trivia Party requires an internet connection to play because it's a real-time multiplayer game. Your connection allows you to play with other players simultaneously.

How are questions selected?

Questions are randomly selected from our trivia database covering various categories including Science, History, Geography, Sports, and Entertainment. Questions are presented fairly to all participants simultaneously.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We use HTTPS encryption, and we don't store sensitive personal data. Most game data is stored locally in your browser. Please review our Privacy Policy for complete information.

Who can I report inappropriate content to?

If you encounter inappropriate questions or content, please email us at safety@triviagame.com with details. We take community safety seriously.

How do I delete my game data?

Your game scores are stored locally in your browser. Simply clear your browser's cache and cookies to delete all local data. We don't store personal information on our servers related to gameplay.

How do I reset my username?

Your username is temporary and tied to your game session. You can change it each time you create or join a new game by entering a new name.

Can I host private games?

Yes! Every game room is private by default. Only players with the room code can join your game. You control who plays with you.

Is there an age restriction?

Trivia Party is suitable for ages 13+. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). Parents are encouraged to supervise younger children's internet usage.

Do you offer API access or integrations?

Currently, we do not offer public API access. If you're interested in partnerships or integrations, contact partnerships@triviagame.com.
